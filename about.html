About Me

Hi, I'm Mahima — a passionate and detail-oriented web developer with a strong focus on creating responsive, accessible, and high-performance websites.

My journey into web development began with a fascination for how design and logic come together to build experiences that people interact with daily. Over time, I’ve built a solid foundation in technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, with a growing interest in front-end frameworks and full-stack development.

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) at Padma Kanya Multiple Campus, affiliated with Tribhuvan University. My academic journey, paired with personal projects, has helped me gain hands-on experience in building clean, interactive, and user-friendly web applications.

I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to continuously grow as a developer. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code, focusing on usability and performance, and keeping up with evolving web standards and best practices.

My goals include:

  • Becoming a well-rounded full-stack developer capable of building complete web solutions from the ground up
  • Collaborating on impactful projects that solve real-world problems
  • Contributing to open-source communities and learning from other developers
  • Gaining professional experience through internships or junior developer roles

Eventually mentoring others and leading development teams with empathy and technical confidence.

I’m excited about the future, open to new challenges, and eager to contribute to projects that make a meaningful impact. All I need is the right opportunity to grow, build, and thrive — one line of code at a time.
